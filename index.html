Asia Pharma

Our story started in 2002 with the formation of Asia Pharma, a new company proudly standing for a good and healthy community and one committed to working together to make for a healthy Afghanistan. Today, we provide the tools and resources to benefit our community and our customers around the region. . Asia Pharma is owned and operated independently by our President, Dr. A. Jalil Saber and Vice President Eng. Rasool Rasooli. With a combined 30 years of experience in the healthcare and pharmaceuticals field, Asia Pharma’s leadership understands the complex and shifting demands of the market.
